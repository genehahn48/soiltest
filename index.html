The map uses data from the University of Kentucky Soil Testing Lab. There are 29 years of data representing 889,912 samples with an average of 30,700 sample tested annually. The AGR-1 Lime and Nutrient Recommendations was used to select croptypes and to bracket the ranges for the categories for each crop. The median value for phosphorus(P) and potassium(K) was calculated for each county and croptype. The ranges across croptypes ied some so the legend is using simpified scale for all croptypes (visit AGR-1 link above for details).

Map design by Gene Hahn for master's in Digital Mapping at the Univeristy of Kentucky.
